• The Unseen Crisis

30% of Land Degraded

Nearly one-third of India's land is losing its health, affecting food security and our environment.

Main Causes

Deforestation, improper farming, and overgrazing leave our soil exposed and vulnerable to erosion.

Severe Consequences

This leads to reduced crop yields, increased flooding, water pollution, and impacts on climate change.

• Our Solution: Plantation

Planting trees is nature's own method for healing and protecting the land. Here‚Äôs how it works.

Roots: Nature's Glue

Tree roots create a dense web that holds soil particles together, preventing erosion from wind and rain.

Canopy: Nature's Umbrella

The leaves and branches intercept heavy rainfall, softening its impact on the ground below.

Fallen Leaves: Nature's Food

As leaves decay, they enrich the soil with nutrients, making it healthier and more fertile.
